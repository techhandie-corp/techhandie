Technical Assistance

At Your Side

Most small business owners have neither the time nor manpower to devote to learning about the various technologies and keeping up with the constant changes in the industry. You need to focus on what you do best: your core competencies; e.g., if you are a baker, your core competency is making baked goods. However, we can not do without technology today, especially if you are a small business or nonprofit. Technology can help you "do much with little". Technology can give your business an edge and help you compete.

TechHandie comes alongside your business - not with a "one size fits all" or "the latest is the greatest" approach - but by taking the time to understand your business and what technologies will provide the right fit. Here are some areas of assistance.

Adhoc Reporting

Ad hoc reports are customized reports generated on demand. From the necessary data and criteria of interest, you can get pretty much any report you would like.

Data Anslysis

Data analysis is the collection, organization, and assessment of data in reflecting, revealing, and/or illuminating some aspect of your business. Data analysis helps to drive sound decision making and strategic thinking. You can get assistance in asking the right questions that help address the "what happened", the "what is happening", and the "what will happen". Coupled with the right tools and techniques, you get meaningful answers you need.

Marketing & Promotional Materials

Marketing and promotional materials designed for your organization - e.g. brochures and flyers

Search Engine Optimization

Improve the quality and quantity of traffic to your website by improving your search engine ranking results and attracting traffic you don't have to pay for in ads.
